Our Programs

At The Bead Bubble, our programs are designed to spark creativity, build confidence, and foster genuine connections between makers of all skill levels. Each offering is free, inclusive, and hands-on—because learning by doing is the heart of what we do.

Beginner Sessions: Start Your Sparkle

These sessions are perfect for newcomers curious about jewellery making. You'll learn essential techniques like wire wrapping, stringing, and basic metal work, all while working with quality tools and materials—all provided free of charge. Instructors guide you step by step, ensuring you feel confident as you explore your creative side.

Advanced Workshops: Master Your Craft

For experienced makers ready to elevate their skills, our advanced sessions cover specialized topics such as stone setting, metal soldering, and wire detailing. Each workshop is led by professional jewelers who share insider tips and advanced methods used in the trade.
