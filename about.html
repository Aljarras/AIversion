About Us!

Behind every class, event, and creative session at The Bead Bubble is a passionate team of makers who believe that art thrives when shared. We're a collective of jewellers, volunteers, and community organizers who came together with a single goal: to make jewellery making accessible, inspiring, and completely free for everyone. Our team includes both experienced artisans and enthusiastic hobbyists who dedicate their time to teaching, organizing, and supporting members. Whether they're leading a workshop, setting up supplies, or greeting you with a smile at the door, each team member helps create the warm, inclusive environment that defines our club.

When you join The Bead Bubble, you become part of this vision—a community that values learning, laughter, and the handmade spirit.

Our Sponsors

We're deeply grateful to our sponsors, whose generosity allows us to keep our programs entirely free. Their contributions help us provide top-quality tools, professional instruction, and the refreshments that make every meeting feel like a creative party.

Together, our team and sponsors form the backbone of a community that celebrates creativity without barriers. We believe that anyone can make something beautiful when given the opportunity and encouragement to try.

At The Bead Bubble, every bead, wire, and gemstone is part of a bigger story—yours. Join us, and let's create something extraordinary together.
