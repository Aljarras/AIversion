About the Club

The Bead Bubble is home to creative souls. We believe that anyone can make captivating jewellery they are proud of given the resources to do so. Whether you've just purchased your first round nose pliers or have recently perfected your magnum opus, we welcome you with open arms. We will be hosting show and tells and bringing in guest jewellers to run instruction sessions (supplies included!).

Our mission is to inspire, empower, and connect people through the art of jewellery making. Creativity should never be limited by cost or experience level, so all of our programs and events are completely free. We provide materials, tools, and expert guidance so that every member—beginner or expert—can learn, grow, and shine.

Our Mission

To provide a welcoming, completely free community and high-quality resources that empower everyone, from absolute beginners to master artisans, to explore and excel in the art of jewellery making.

Membership in the Bead Bubble is your all-access pass to instruction, inspiration, and connection—and it's 100% free!

We host regular events designed to elevate your craft and connect you with fellow makers.

Guest Jeweller Instruction Sessions: Learn specialized techniques from professional artisans. All supplies, materials, and tools are provided free of charge.

Show & Tell Events: Share your latest creations, get feedback, and celebrate the work of others in a supportive atmosphere.

The Bead Bubble is built on curiosity, community, and collaboration. We encourage experimentation, celebrate every milestone, and create a space where everyone feels comfortable expressing themselves. No matter where you are on your creative journey, you'll find support, laughter, and inspiration waiting for you here.

At The Bead Bubble, every bead, wire, and gemstone is part of a bigger story—yours. Join us, and let's create something extraordinary together.
