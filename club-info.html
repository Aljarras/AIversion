Apply for a Membership Today!

Becoming a member of The Bead Bubble is simple, fun, and 100% free. Once you join, you'll unlock access to a wide range of resources, events, and opportunities designed to help you learn, grow, and connect with fellow makers.

Beyond events, membership connects you to a vibrant, ongoing community.

You'll be part of a supportive network where people inspire and motivate one another. Many of our members form lasting friendships, collaborate on side projects, or even start creative ventures together.

At The Bead Bubble, the benefits go beyond skill-building—they're about belonging.

You'll gain access to knowledge, confidence, and a sense of connection that keeps you coming back. Whether you attended every event or just drop in when inspiration strikes, you're always welcome here.

What we Offer

We host Show & Tell Events, casual gatherings where members share their projects, discuss challenges, and exchange feedback in a warm, encouraging setting. It's a great way to celebrate progress, discover new ideas, and gain confidence in your work. Every meeting includes complimentary snacks and refreshments, because creativity flows best when you're comfortable, relaxed, and well-fed!

Members are invited to attend Guest Jeweller Instruction Sessions, where professional artisans share specialized skills—from intricate beadwork to metal forming and polishing.

All materials, tools, and supplies are provided at no cost, so you can focus entirely on your craft.
